Research Consultancy Services

Fine Touch Research advises and consults Bachelor’s, Master’s, and PhD students throughout the entire research process. Our services include:

  1. Selecting and refining research topics within the student’s field of study
  2. Defining a clear and researchable problem statement and purpose statement
  3. Formulating main research objectives
  4. Developing logically linked specific objectives, research questions, and hypotheses
  5. Conducting critical and analytical literature review and synthesis
  6. Choosing appropriate qualitative, quantitative, or mixed research methodology
  7. Collecting, coding, preparing, and analysing research data and reporting results
  8. Interpreting and discussing results and making policy, program, and research recommendations
